Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the pilot and implementation:

How does the AI integrate with our SAP instances? +

We connect via SAP's standard APIs (RFC/BAPI or OData depending on your configuration). The AI reads data from SAP but doesn't modify your master data—invoice drafts are created separately and only posted after your team approves them. We'll work with your IT team during Discovery to configure secure API access.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake on an invoice? +

Every AI-generated invoice draft goes through a human review step via the Power Apps interface. Your Finance team can approve, reject, or modify any draft before it's posted. The AI learns from corrections over time, improving accuracy. We're targeting 80%+ accuracy during the pilot, meaning 8 out of 10 invoices require no changes.

Can we handle multiple SAP instances? +

Yes. We understand you operate three SAP instances in North America (two legacy R/3 and one S/4). The pilot will focus on the S/4 instance initially, with the architecture designed to extend to the R/3 systems in Phase 2 if the pilot is successful.

What if we don't hit the target metrics? +

The 12-week pilot is designed to validate the approach with real data and real processes. At week 12, we'll have clear metrics on accuracy, cycle time, and deflection rates. If we don't hit targets, you'll understand exactly where the gaps are and can make an informed decision about next steps. There's no long-term commitment—the pilot is designed to de-risk the investment.

Who from MAHLE needs to be involved? +

We'll need engagement from: (1) Finance team members who handle invoicing and vendor inquiries—for requirements and UAT, (2) IT team—for SAP API access and environment setup, (3) An executive sponsor—for decisions and removing blockers. We estimate 5-10 hours per week from your team during the pilot, with the heaviest involvement during Discovery (weeks 1-2) and UAT (weeks 6-8).

How do you handle data security? +

All data stays within your cloud environment or ours (depending on deployment option). We use encrypted connections for all SAP API calls, and no invoice or vendor data is stored outside the processing pipeline. If you choose in-house deployment, everything remains in your environment. We can provide detailed security documentation during the MSA process.

What's the long-term cost after the pilot? +

If the pilot is successful and you want to scale, we'll provide a Phase 2 proposal with options for: (1) Continued Provectus management as a managed service, (2) Knowledge transfer to your team with support agreement, or (3) Hybrid model. We'll scope Phase 2 pricing based on actual pilot results and your scaling requirements.
